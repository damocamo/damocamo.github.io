About the game

Foot Run is a fast, lane‑based endless runner where every step matters. Swipe to change lanes, avoid obstacles, grab golden coins and use special powers from unique shoes to push for new high scores.

Gameplay

  • Easy to learn: Simple left and right swipe controls to move between lanes.
  • Hard to master: Obstacles appear faster and patterns get trickier as you progress.
  • Endless levels: Run through many stages with increasing speed and difficulty.

Collect unique shoes

Unlock different pairs of shoes, each with its own look and special abilities. The better you run, the more coins you earn – and the more shoes you can add to your collection.

Achievements & stats

  • Unlock achievements for distance, coins, perfect runs and more.
  • Track your personal bests and progress with detailed statistics.
  • Earn rewards for completing challenges and pushing your limits.
